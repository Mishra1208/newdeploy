    GRADUATION ELIGIBILITY INFORMATION

     GRADUATION ELIGIBILITY INFORMATION 

    Please see the appropriate section of the Undergraduate Calendar for the year you started your current program.

    You must have a last annual GPA of at least 2.00 as well as a Cumulative GPA of at least 2.00 to be eligible to graduate.

    You should also comply with all messages that appear on your transcript. Should you have any outstanding conditions from previous years, they must be satisfied prior to graduation.

    Graduation does NOT occur automatically, and you must apply for graduation.

    Graduation Application Deadlines:

    • January 15 (for Spring Convocation) if you will be completing all requirements by the end of the winter term.

    • July 1 (for Fall Convocation) if you will be completing all requirements by the end of summer term.
