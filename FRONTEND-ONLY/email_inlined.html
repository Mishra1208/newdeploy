Chrome extension
After installing the extension, your Visual Schedule Builder gets two superpowers. First, RateMyProfessors ratings appear right next to each prof — no more alt tabbing. Second, a one click button exports your entire schedule to Google Calendar, including all academic dates. It also syncs your unofficial transcript to your ConU Planner dashboard automatically.
how it works: Install once. The extension injects the RMP scores directly into VSB pages, adds the “Sync to Calender” button, and when you view your unofficial transcript, a new “sync to dashboard” button appears. click it — done.
“registration just got an upgrade.” see RateMyProf scores inside VSB, 1 click export full schedule + academic dates to Google Calendar, 1 click transcript sync.
Grade distribution & insights
After syncing your transcript, you unlock course level grade distributions — like the one here for COMP 352. See average GPA, pass/fail rates, and a bar chart of every letter grade. We also add a quick analysis: hardest term, easiest term, highest intake. No more guessing how curved a class really is.
how it works: Once your transcript is synced, any course you’ve taken shows a “grade distribution” tab. built from hundreds of real student records.
“stop guessing your GPA.” see actual grade distributions, per term difficulty, and trends.
Prerequisite tech tree
Pick any course (like COMP 346 here) and the tree shows you everything you need before it — and every future class it unlocks. Visual, zoomable, and much clearer than sifting through the academic calendar. No more missed prerequisites that delay graduation.
how it works: Search a course, and the tree expands. Prerequisites on the left, courses that depend on it on the right. Drag to pan, scroll to zoom.
“never take the wrong class again.” see full prerequisite chains, spot missing links, plan years ahead.
Smart schedule planner
Your personal drag and drop planner. Add courses, see total credits (16 here), and organise by semester. Save multiple draft schedules, mix and match sections, and export to CSV when you’re ready. Works independently of VSB, so you can plan even when VSB is slow.
how it works: Search courses, drag them into Fall/Winter/Summer containers. The credit count updates live. Create a free account to save drafts.
“beat the VSB crash. plan ahead.” draft perfect schedules, save them securely, export to csv.
Automated seat finder
Real time seat availability for any course. Pick a term, subject, and course number — you’ll see every section with its current status (OPEN, waitlist count, closed). No more refreshing the class finder every ten minutes.
how it works: Just select term, subject, and course number. The table shows section, schedule, class number, and live seat status. Updated constantly.
“class full? we’ve got your back.” real time capacities, open/closed/waitlisted. snipe your spot the moment it frees up.
