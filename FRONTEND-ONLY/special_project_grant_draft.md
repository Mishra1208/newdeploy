# Special Project Grant Application Draft
**Project:** ConU Planner

---

## 1. Project Statement (100 words max)
**ConU Planner** is a comprehensive, student-built academic platform designed to solve the complexity of degree planning at Concordia. While official tools are often fragmented, ConU Planner unifies course discovery, prerequisite visualization, professor analytics, and schedule building into a single, premium interface. Our mission is to empower students with data-driven insights—like grade distributions and "Rate My Professors" integration—and intuitive tools like the "Seat Finder" and "VSB Calendar Exporter." It is an open-source initiative built by students, for students, ensuring that academic success starts with a clear, stress-free plan.

---

## 2. Project Description (400 words max)
Academic planning is often the most stressful part of a student's journey. Information is scattered across the Undergraduate Calendar, SIS, various department websites, and external forums. **ConU Planner** centralizes this ecosystem into a modern, high-performance web application.

**Key Features & Innovation:**
*   **Visual Prerequisite Tree**: Unlike text-based lists, our interactive node graph allows students to visualize their entire degree progression, identifying bottlenecks and critical paths instantly.
*   **Advanced Course Search**: A lightning-fast search engine indexing over 7,900+ courses, filterable by level, department, and prerequisites, with immediate access to course descriptions.
*   **Professor Analytics**: We seamlessly integrate "Rate My Professors" data, offering students crucial context on difficulty and quality directly alongside course listings. Our localized analytics dashboard compares professors side-by-side to aid decision-making.
*   **Seat Finder & Alerts**: A real-time tool helping students track distinct course sections, alerting them when "Closed" classes open up, democratizing access to high-demand courses.
*   **VSB Integration**: We recently developed a browser extension that bridges the gap between Concordia's Visual Schedule Builder and personal calendars, allowing one-click exports to Google/Apple Calendar.

**Community Impact:**
The project is built on a "Community First" philosophy. We maintain an open feedback loop via Reddit and direct user forms, implementing features requested by the student body. By reducing administrative friction, we allow students to focus more on their education and less on the bureaucracy of enrollment.

**Funding Utilization:**
The Special Project Grant will support the infrastructure costs (server hosting, domain maintenance), database scaling for our 5,000+ active users, and marketing materials to reach first-year students who struggle most with navigating university systems.

---

## 3. Impact on Fine Arts Community (100 words max)
While ConU Planner serves the entire university, it uniquely benefits Fine Arts students who manage complex, irregular schedules involving studio time and workshops. Our **Visual Prerequisite Tree** is particularly intuitive for visual learners, helping them map out intertwined studio requirements. Additionally, our **VSB Export** tool ensures they can synchronize their rigid studio hours with personal calendars to better manage time for creative practice. By simplifying the administrative burden, we empower Fine Arts students to reclaim time for their craft and community engagement.
