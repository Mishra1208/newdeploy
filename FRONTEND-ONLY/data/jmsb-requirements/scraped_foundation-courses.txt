Foundation courses

108-Credit Mature Entry Foundation courses:
ECON 201
ECON 203
MATH 208
MATH 209

+ 6 non-business elective credits

120-Credit Extended Credit Foundation courses:
ECON 201
ECON 203
ENGL 210 or ENGL 212
MATH 208
MATH 209

+ 15 non-business elective credits
