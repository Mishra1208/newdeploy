Notes
Enrolment in this program is limited. The selection process may include recommendations from faculty members as well as interviews of applicants to this program.
Students must maintain a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.3 in their Accountancy Courses and achieve a minimum grade of B+ in ACCO 496 and ACCO 497 to remain in the honours program. Students who are withdrawn from the honours program may proceed in the Major in Accountancy.
